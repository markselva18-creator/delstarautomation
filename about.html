Who We Are

Engineers Beyond Engineering

Delstar Automation Solutions is a multifaceted engineering company focused on Automation, Embedded Automotive Electronics, EV Solutions, Industry 4.0 and Fabrication Works.

Based in Hosur, Tamil Nadu — at the heart of India's manufacturing corridor — we serve clients across automotive, EV, pharma, food & beverage, textile and chemical industries with turnkey automation solutions.

Our team combines deep domain expertise with hands-on engineering capability, delivering projects from initial analysis through design, implementation, testing and commissioning.
