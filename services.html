Detailed Services

What We Deliver

Analysis

We begin with thorough process analysis — studying your production workflow, identifying bottlenecks, and conducting feasibility studies to define the optimal automation strategy for your specific requirements.

Design

Our engineering team creates detailed mechanical, electrical and software designs — including 3D modeling, control system architecture, PLC/HMI programming specifications and comprehensive documentation.

Implementation

Precision fabrication and assembly of automation equipment in our facility — including machining, welding, wiring, panel building and system integration with rigorous in-process quality checks.

Factory Testing

Comprehensive Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT) at our facility — validating all functional requirements, performance benchmarks and safety systems before equipment leaves our premises.

Field Installation

Expert on-site installation by our trained engineers — including mechanical setup, electrical connections, network integration and alignment with existing production infrastructure.

Commissioning

Full commissioning and Site Acceptance Testing (SAT) — operator training, documentation handover, production trial runs and ongoing support to ensure seamless integration into your operations.
